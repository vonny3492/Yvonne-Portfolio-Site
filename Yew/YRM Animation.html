Explanation Animation

Our assignment was to take a meme, or colloquial term for modern day phenomenon and explain it. I chose planking, the term used to define people randomly laying flat on incredibly random surfaces and documenting it for others. I thought it was the most interesting option when choosing from slugging, jaywalking and flashmobs. I wanted to create an interesting movie, with a detailed background. I wanted to use comedy and a sort of zaniness which is parallel to the essence of planking. I had the important words be very large and moving, as well as bright colors. I used gradients a consistent color theme through the video. I also liked the idea of using a simple stick figure because it contrasted interestingly with the background.
