Hello! I'm Yvonne, a graphic designer with a strong UX focus.

While I grew up in Madison I went up nort' to the University of Minnesota to earn my BFA and spend a wonderful semester in Scotland. Since moving back home I’ve gained professional experience in usability and graphic design all while caring for my frolicsome ferrets and delightful doggo.

I'm passionate about problem solving in ways that work best for everyone. I think usability research, precise layout, and smart typography are key to great design.

I'm keen about reading, goat farms, pottery, television, organizing, and work life balance.
